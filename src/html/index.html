FEATURES

Core features & Integrated trading tools

Our advanced interface offers an all-in-one solution for cryptocurrency traders, professional fund managers and everyone in between.

Multi-exchange trading

Synchronize your exchanges and wallets data while trading on multiple exchanges from a single secured account. Trading history and portfolio balances are automatically imported and updated, allowing you to review previous trades and track balances across all connected exchanges.

Portfolio tracking

Your portfolio balance aggregates balance data from all connected exchanges and wallets. We also support sub-accounts such as: Margin, Lending, Swap etc. In addition you can add and manage manual balance entries to represent offline wallets or cold storage.

Multi-charting interface

Fully customizable multi-charting interface with real-time market data from all major crypto exchanges. You can simultaneously monitor prices of different currencies and use more than 75 technical indicators and drawing tools.

Multi-portfolio manager

Manage multiple portfolios and share them with colleagues and clients. Each portfolio provides detailed list of crypto assets and various performance indicators. You can edit past statistical data and manually input balance entries in each portfolio. You can also receive automated portfolio reports by email.

Advanced trade orders

“Stop” and “Stop-Limit” are advanced order types that help you protect your capital from higher losses when price suddenly falls.

Smart trading panel

Out smart trading panel combines flexible field calculations, order depth indicator and other smart features to help you place your orders with ease.

Market scanner

Compare various performance indicators for 10,000+ markets across all supported exchanges and quickly find trading opportunities.
